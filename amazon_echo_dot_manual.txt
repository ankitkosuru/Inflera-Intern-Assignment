Amazon Echo Dot (5th Gen) – Quick Start Guide

1. Setting Up Your Device
- Plug the Echo Dot into a power outlet using the included 15W adapter.
- Wait for the light ring to turn orange. This indicates the device is in setup mode.
- Open the Amazon Alexa app on your smartphone and follow the on-screen instructions to connect the Echo Dot to Wi-Fi.

2. Connecting to Wi-Fi
- In the Alexa app, go to Devices > Add Device > Amazon Echo > Echo Dot.
- Select your Wi-Fi network and enter the password.
- Once connected, Alexa will confirm with a greeting.

3. Using Voice Commands
- Say “Alexa” followed by a command. Examples:
  * “Alexa, what’s the weather today?”
  * “Alexa, play music.”
  * “Alexa, set a timer for 10 minutes.”

4. Smart Home Setup
- To connect smart bulbs, plugs, or other devices, say: “Alexa, discover devices.”
- Configure devices in the Alexa app under Devices > Add Device.

5. Troubleshooting
- If the Echo Dot does not connect to Wi-Fi, reset it by pressing and holding the Action button for 20 seconds.
- Restart the device if it becomes unresponsive by unplugging and plugging it back in.
